Last Trip: Luxembourg

During my recent trip to Luxembourg, I was captivated by the country's unique blend of picturesque landscapes and historical heritage. Luxembourg City charmed me with its harmonious mix of ancient architecture and modern institutions, including medieval fortifications and contemporary European establishments. The lush green valleys and hilltop castles created a magical atmosphere. It was an enriching experience that left me eager to further explore this multifaceted country.
